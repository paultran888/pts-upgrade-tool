## Cold Outreach — Audit-First Approach

### Template 1: The Score Drop
> I ran a quick audit on [business name]'s website and it scored [XX]/100. The biggest issue? [specific finding — e.g. "no mobile viewport, so over 60% of your visitors are seeing a broken layout"]. Here's the full report: [audit link with ?url= param]. Happy to chat if you want to fix it.

### Template 2: The Competitor Gap
> I noticed [competitor name]'s website loads in 1.2 seconds — yours takes [X]s. At that speed, you're likely losing over half your visitors before they even see what you offer. I put together a free audit here: [audit link]. No strings attached.

### Template 3: The Revenue Leak
> Quick question — have you checked your website recently on a phone? I noticed [specific issue] which means mobile visitors (60%+ of traffic) are getting a rough experience. I ran a free scan: [audit link]. Might be worth a look.

### Template 4: The Shame Hook (for DMs/casual outreach)
> Not trying to be rude, but your website doesn't match the quality of your [product/service/work]. I ran a quick score on it — [XX]/100. The good news: the fixes are straightforward. Want me to show you what it would look like upgraded?

## Social Media Posts — Symptom-First

### Hook 1: The Embarrassment
> Your website is the first thing potential customers see. If you're embarrassed to share your URL, that's a problem you can't afford to ignore.

### Hook 2: Traffic With No Calls
> Getting traffic to your website but your phone isn't ringing? Here's what's probably happening: visitors land, don't see a clear next step, and leave. No CTA = no conversions. (Free audit link in bio)

### Hook 3: The $5K Quote
> An agency quoted you $5,000+ and 6-8 weeks for a website redesign. What if you could see a live preview of the fix in 30 seconds — for free?

### Hook 4: The Speed Kill
> Your website takes 6 seconds to load. 53% of visitors leave after 3. You're losing half your potential customers before they even see what you do. (Check your speed free — link in bio)

### Hook 5: The Competitor Angle
> Your competitor's website loads fast, looks modern, and has clear CTAs. Yours... doesn't. Customers aren't loyal — they go wherever the experience is better.

### Hook 6: The Hidden Problem
> Most business owners don't know their website is broken on mobile. They designed it on a laptop, checked it on a laptop, and never opened it on a phone. 60% of their traffic is mobile. That's 60% getting a terrible experience.

### Hook 7: The Cost of Inaction
> Every day your outdated website is live, it's actively losing you money. Not hypothetically — literally. Visitors bounce, competitors win, and you never even know it happened.

### Hook 8: The Not Secure Warning
> If your website says "Not Secure" in the browser bar, 82% of people will leave immediately. You might as well put a "CLOSED" sign on your digital front door.
